The Radar

The same super-resolution radar that powers the Xtreme Weather app, ported to the web. Pick any station, layer on warnings and reports, and watch the storm live right from your browser.

Open the radar

What you get

  1. A true port of the app

    Super-res reflectivity and velocity, warning polygons drawn where they actually are, and 150+ stations to pick from. The experience the iOS app delivers, running in your browser.

  2. Flat map or full globe

    Track on a flat map or spin the planet in 3D, with live lightning, spotter positions, mPING reports, and METAR stations layered on top.

  3. Built for coverage

    When a Coverage Mode activates, the radar is where the room watches together. Follow the storm live while our Coverage Experts break down what you are seeing.
