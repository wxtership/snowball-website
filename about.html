About Us

Weather it. Snowball it.

Snowball is the most advanced Discord app for weather: AI that actually fetches real data, a full economy system, radio, music, and so much more. It's all built for one place, the Xtreme Weather community, where forecasts, storms, and a whole lot of fun live under one roof.
